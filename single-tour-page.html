Go on horseback to the mountains of Paraiso. Visit, accompained by a local guide, the small farms of the locals living in the municipality of "villa Nizao" where bananas, coffee,cocoa and avocados are grown.

The tour starts early in the morning with the ascent to the mountains of Paraiso. The path leads, past small communities, up to villa Nizao(small mountain village). During the ascent we will make some stops to admire the panormaic views over the coast of Paraiso and to explore the communities of Dominican and Halitian farmers on our way.

The way back leads through the fresh and clear water of the river "Nizaito", back to the village of the Paraiso. On the way down we make another stop where you have the chance to visit a greenhouse where paprika is grown. Finally, we return to the village where we leave the horses behind and take the road back to your hotel by car.
