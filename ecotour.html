Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Paradise Andaman

Both Andaman and Nicobar are home to about 300 islands and are known for their beautiful white-sand beaches, tropical rainforests, mangrove rainforests, and palm-lined beaches.The only volcano in India, Barren Island, is located in Andaman and Nicobar.


Beautiful coral reefs are also present here which house Sharks, Rays, and all sorts of marine life thus a paradise for diving and snorkeling.About 270 species of birds are found in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, 14 species of which are endemic.

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a union territory of India consisting of 572 islands, of which 37 are inhabited, at the junction of the Bay of Bengal and the Andaman Sea.The territory's capital is the city of Port Blair.
